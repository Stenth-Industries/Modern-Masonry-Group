architect, engineer, and designer Services

Helping you build responsibly throughout the project lifecycle.

We care about quality and craft as much as you do. One of our passions is helping you bridge design and constructability so that your project comes to life just as you’ve envisioned it.

FREE TECHNICAL SUPPORT​

IMI’s free project support, technical assistance, and education is here to help you at any stage in your building’s lifecycle.

Contact us and we’ll connect you with one of our experts to get started. Our multidisciplinary team draws on decades of experience developing solutions for high-performing masonry and tile projects.
